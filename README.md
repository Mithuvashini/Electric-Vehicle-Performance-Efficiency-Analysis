#  📌 Electric-Vehicle-Performance-Efficiency-Analysis-For-Optimal-Consumer-Selection

- This project, **Smart Electric Vehicle Selection for Consumers Analysis**, provides a complete data-driven evaluation of electric vehicles across multiple brands, seating capacities, and price categories.

- The analysis was performed using key performance indicators such as **battery capacity, driving range, charging time, efficiency score, acceleration, top speed, safety rating, and pricing segments** to help consumers make informed vehicle purchase decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze EV market performance across multiple brands
- Compare vehicle efficiency and range
- Evaluate price-based categories
- Identify top-performing models by seating capacity
- Provide data-driven recommendations for consumers

---

## 📊 Dataset Features
The dataset includes the following columns:

- Manufacturer
- Model
- Vehicle Type
- Drive Type
- Fuel Type
- Battery Capacity (kWh)
- Range (km)
- Charging Time (min)
- Fast Charging Support
- Release Year
- Country
- City
- Seating Capacity
- Price (USD)
- Efficiency Score
- Acceleration (0–100 km/h)
- Top Speed
- Warranty Years
- Cargo Space

---

### 📊 Overall Project Findings

- **10 manufacturers** and **11 EV models** were analyzed
- Vehicles were compared across **Economy, Standard, and Luxury** categories
- Average battery capacity was **75.02 kWh**
- Average driving range was **374.77 km**
- Average charging time was **64.59 minutes**
- **49% of vehicles met the high-efficiency target**
- Luxury vehicles consistently outperformed other categories in **speed, safety, and overall performance score**

### 📈 Key Business Insights

- Premium EV brands dominate the **luxury market segment**
- Standard vehicles provide the best **balance between cost and performance**
- Economy models focus on **affordability and efficiency**
- **Battery range and charging speed** are the most critical factors influencing consumer decisions
- Performance competition is extremely close across all segments, indicating a highly competitive EV market

---

### 🎯 Final Recommendation

Based on the overall performance score and comparative analysis:

- **EV6** emerges as one of the strongest all-round performers in premium segments
- **ID.4** performs exceptionally well in economy and standard segments
- **Model S / Model X** remain strong premium contenders
- **Leaf and Ioniq** deliver excellent efficiency-focused performance

This project demonstrates how **data analytics, visualization, and comparative performance scoring** can support smart consumer decision-making in the growing electric vehicle market.
